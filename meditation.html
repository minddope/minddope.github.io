Meditation

Wake up in the morning, sit down and listen to the reality spreading like a fan.
Someone is starting the car outside.
The neighbour downstairs is listening to the news.
The birds are chirping. The clouds are floating in the sky.
The sun is heating up the soil.
Someone is falling asleep on the other side of the globe.
Someone is reaching the summit of a mountain, someone is reading a book.
The pages of the book are rustling and the leaves are rustling in the woods.
The animals are starting the day, the humans are starting the day.
The trees are growing, the children are growing.
Someone’s life is coming to an end, someone’s is beginning.
The vibration is wandering the world constantly.
You are seated here and you are listening,
and everything is flowing through your body and heart.
And you are everywhere and nowhere.
For a moment you are tuned to this unique morning on Earth.
There’s no perfect silence, but nothing is perfect.
Perfect things cannot change.
Transformation is the essence of life.
